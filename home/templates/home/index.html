The intricate SCP universe like you have never seen before

Originally launched 13 years ago the SCP universe have been growing and evolving thanks to the collaboration and cooperation of writers around the world, with more than five thousand articles the SCP universe have become one project of itâ€™s own kind.

From all types of supernatural stories, combining interdimensional creatures, aliens, lovecraftian themes or other tales that make us think about the very basic human nature the SCP foundation has something for everyone.

The impact in pop culture is also one the biggest achievements of this project, from video-games to short movies the SCP universe expands itself to new territories, including (finally) books.

The SCP universe finally in your bookcase

The first stories that will be publishing are Volume 1 (The SCP Foundation) and Volume 2 (The G.O.C. Chronicles).

Volume 1 is an introduction dedicated to those that already know the SCP universe but want to have a better understanding of what the SCP Foundation is, its procedures and the different terminology generally used in the universe.

Volume 2 is the first of the books dedicated to one of the organizations that live alongside the Foundation. The Global Occult Coalition (G.O.C) is presented as the opposite side of the same coin, both organizations have the same objective protect humankind. But while the Foundation thinks that is better to Secure, Contain and Protect any anomaly to study it, the G.O.C. has usually a more direct approach to the situation, shoot first ask question later.

And this is just the begging, coming soon news volumes will be released. We think that some stories would make more sense released in compilations, but there are also some articles that deserver their own books. For now we leave which one will be the next to your imagination...
